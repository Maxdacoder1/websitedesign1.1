Materials

  • Creeper template
  • Colored cardstock
  • White cardstock
  • Glue stick
  • Scissors
  • 1 1/2 inch square paper punch

Instructions

  1. Print and cut out the creeper template. We trimmed ours with a paper cutter, but kids can just use scissors.
  2. Use the paper punch to cut out a large pile of squares. Use a variety of colors so your child can give their creeper a classic pixelated look. Your child will need 8 black squares and 17 green and gray squares. Alternatively, kids can simply paint or color their squares.
  3. White cardstock
  4. Invite your child to design their creeper.
  5. Your Paper Creeper Craft is complete.
