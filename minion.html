Minion Pencil Stand Craft Tutorial

Materials Needed:

  • Empty Tin Can
  • Yellow, Blue, and Black Construction Paper
  • White Paper
  • Glue
  • Scissors
  • Black Marker
  • Ruler
  • Pencil
  • Googly Eyes (or white paper and a black marker for the eyes)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Tin Can: Ensure the tin can is clean, dry, and free of sharp edges.
  2. Cover the Can with Yellow Paper: Measure, cut, and glue the yellow paper around the can.
  3. Create the Overalls: Cut and glue a strip of blue paper around the lower part of the can for the overalls.
  4. Add Overall Details: Cut out and glue additional blue paper for the pocket and straps of the overalls.
  5. Make the Goggles: Cut a black paper strip for the goggle strap and attach it, then glue on googly eyes or paper eyes.
  6. Attach the Eyes: Position and glue the eyes over the goggle strap.
  7. Add Final Details: Draw the mouth and hair with a black marker, and add any additional details as desired.
  8. Let it Dry: Allow all glue to dry completely before use.
  9. Use Your Minion Pencil Stand: Place stationery items in the stand.
